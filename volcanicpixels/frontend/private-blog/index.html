Want to make all or part of your WordPress website private? With support for; multiple passwords, upload protection, RSS feed protection, access logs and much more, we've got you covered.

Features

Intuitive interface

We've made it easy to get started straight away.

Multiple passwords

Define as many passwords as you want. This makes tracking logins, removing people's access easy.

By default you can set up to 10 passwords, this can be increased by changing $maxPasswords in plugin.php

Choose what to protect

You can choose individual pages or posts, categories, tags, post-types or url patterns to protect.
